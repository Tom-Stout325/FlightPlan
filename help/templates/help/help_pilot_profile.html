Pilot Profile Help

Step-by-Step Guide
  1. Register for an account by creating a username, and password.
  2. After registering, you'll be redirected to the Login page where you will login for the first time.
  3. After you login you will be directed to the Drone Portal. First click on the Edit button in the Your Information section.
  4. Fill out your information details:
    • First and Last Name
    • Email Address
  5. Next, click on the Edit button in the Pilot Credentials section.
  6. Fill out your information details:
    • Pilot License Number (optional)
    • License Date (optional)
    • Upload an image of your Pilot License (optional)
  7. Click Save to store your profile.
  8. You can edit your profile later by visiting the Profile section from the menu.
  9. Your Flight Summary section will be automatically completed when you upload flight logs.
